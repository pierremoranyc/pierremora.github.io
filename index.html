About Me

Hello! I'm Pierre Mora, a passionate Data Analyst with a rich background in marketing and a profound enthusiasm for unlocking the narratives hidden within data.

Beyond the realms of Python, Power BI, Tableau, and SQL, where I excel in turning complex datasets into compelling visual stories and strategic insights, I find joy in exploring the intersection of data and everyday life.

Transitioning from a professional background in marketing to the realm of data analysis, I discovered the pivotal role of data in shaping marketing strategies. My experience in marketing unveiled to me how strategies are often grounded in data-driven insights. This realization ignited my desire to delve deeper into the field, to not just rely on prepared visuals or reports, but to engage directly with the raw data. Understanding the market from its data roots has been a journey of discovery. My marketing expertise complements my data skills, offering a unique advantage in interpreting data with a business-focused lens.

In my free time, you'll find me indulging in calisthenics, video editing, driving sports cars, and of course, coding for fun, which provide a creative outlet and keep my analytical skills sharp.

I'm currently furthering my expertise through a Master's in Data Science, which has been an exhilarating adventure of growth and learning.

My goal is to leverage my skills in data analysis to drive meaningful change and innovation. Whether it's through uncovering hidden insights or helping shape data-driven strategies, I am dedicated to making a significant impact in the field.

My Project Portfolio

Showcasing a Blend of Advanced Analytics and Machine Learning Expertise.

LinkedIn IT Job Market Analysis - Python and Tableau Insights, August 2023

In this project, I've conducted a comprehensive analysis of the IT job market using over 12,000 job postings from LinkedIn, dated August 2023. Employing Python for data cleaning and advanced analytics, including regression and clustering, the study reveals key trends in job availability, geographic distribution, salary ranges, and required skills in the IT sector. The insights are vividly presented through interactive Tableau visualizations, offering an in-depth look at the dynamics of the IT industry's job market.

Instacart Customer Behavior Analysis - Python-Driven Insights

In this exploratory project, I delve into Instacart's customer data to uncover key insights into purchasing behaviors and patterns. Utilizing Python and its powerful libraries like Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, and SciPy, the analysis is conducted through Jupyter Notebooks. The primary focus is on identifying the busiest shopping days and hours, understanding spending habits throughout the day, categorizing products into simpler price ranges, and determining the popularity of different product types.
